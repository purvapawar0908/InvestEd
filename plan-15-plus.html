Tax Saving Tips for Income Level ₹15 Lakh and Above

Income Level: ₹15 Lakh and Above

Tax Tip: Strategize for High Tax Outflow

With an income of ₹15 lakh or above, your taxable income is subject to a 30% tax rate. At this income level, you need to implement more sophisticated tax-saving strategies to minimize your tax liability. By focusing on high-return, long-term tax-saving options, you can reduce the impact of taxes while securing your financial future.

Investment Plans

Equity-Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS)

High-income earners should prioritize investing in Equity-Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS) due to their dual benefits of tax deduction under Section 80C and potential for higher returns. ELSS investments come with a 3-year lock-in period, making them a suitable option for long-term tax planning.

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Contributing to the National Pension Scheme (NPS) is an excellent way to reduce your taxable income and simultaneously plan for retirement. An additional ₹50,000 deduction is available under Section 80CCD(1B) beyond the ₹1.5 lakh limit of Section 80C, providing significant tax-saving opportunities.

Other Tax Savings

Real Estate Investments

If you have invested in real estate, ensure that you are taking full advantage of deductions for home loan interest under Section 24. You can claim a deduction of up to ₹2 lakh on the interest paid for home loans, which will significantly reduce your taxable income.

Health and Education Deductions

With an income of ₹15 lakh or above, make sure you're using all available deductions for health insurance premiums under Section 80D. Additionally, donations made to approved charitable organizations under Section 80G can also reduce your tax burden.
