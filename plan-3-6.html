Tax Saving Tips for Income Level ₹3 Lakh - ₹6 Lakh

Income Level: ₹3 Lakh - ₹6 Lakh

Tax Tip: Avail of the Basic Exemption + Section 80C Deductions

For individuals earning between ₹3 lakh and ₹6 lakh, you will be taxed at a 5% rate on income above ₹2.5 lakh. To minimize your tax liability, you should utilize tax-saving options like Section 80C deductions and additional deductions for the National Pension Scheme (NPS).

Tax Saving Options

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Contributions to NPS qualify for a tax deduction of up to ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B), over and above the ₹1.5 lakh limit under Section 80C. This is a great way to reduce taxable income while saving for retirement.

Tax-Saving Fixed Deposits

These fixed deposits have a 5-year lock-in period and qualify for deductions under Section 80C. They offer a fixed return and a secure investment option.
