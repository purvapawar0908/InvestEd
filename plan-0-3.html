Saving Tips for Income Level ₹0 - ₹3 Lakh

Income Level: ₹0 - ₹3 Lakh

Tax Tip: Utilize the Basic Exemption Limit

For individuals earning up to ₹3 lakh, no income tax is payable due to the basic exemption limit of ₹2.5 lakh. However, if you're earning slightly above ₹2.5 lakh, you should look into tax-saving options to reduce your taxable income further.

Investment Plans

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

This is an excellent long-term tax-saving tool. Contributions to PPF qualify for a deduction under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh). The interest earned is tax-free, and the maturity proceeds are also exempt from tax.

National Savings Certificate (NSC)

You can invest in NSC under Section 80C. It provides a fixed interest rate, and the interest earned is taxable, but you can claim a tax deduction for the principal and interest under 80C and 80C(2) respectively.

Other Options

Health Insurance (Section 80D)

If you're under 60 years of age, you can claim a deduction of up to ₹25,000 for premiums paid for health insurance policies. For senior citizens (above 60 years), the limit increases to ₹50,000.

Tax Tip: Buying health insurance not only helps protect your health but also helps reduce your taxable income.
