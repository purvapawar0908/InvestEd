Tax Tips & Investment Plans for Income Level ₹7 Lakh - ₹9 Lakh

Income Level: ₹7 Lakh - ₹9 Lakh

Tax Tip: Maximize Use of Deductions

With an income above ₹5 lakh, the tax rate increases to 10%. To reduce your taxable income, it’s essential to utilize available tax-saving options effectively. By making strategic investments, you can lower your tax liability and save more for the future.

Investment Plans

Equity-Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS)

For individuals in the ₹7 lakh to ₹9 lakh income range, investing in ELSS offers tax deduction benefits under Section 80C, along with the potential for higher returns. With a 3-year lock-in period, ELSS is ideal for long-term wealth creation while reducing tax liability.

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Contribute to the National Pension Scheme (NPS) to reduce taxable income and plan for retirement. Deduction of up to ₹1.5 lakh is available under Section 80C, with an additional ₹50,000 available under Section 80CCD(1B) for NPS contributions, making it an excellent tax-saving tool.

Other Tax Savings

Health Insurance Premiums

Health insurance is a vital investment for both your well-being and tax savings. Deduct premiums paid for yourself and your family under Section 80D. This helps in lowering taxable income while securing health coverage for the future.

Home Loan Interest

If you have a home loan, don’t forget to claim deductions on the interest paid under Section 24. This can help reduce your tax burden while also aiding in property ownership and long-term wealth creation.
