Tax Saving Tips for Income Level ₹9 Lakh - ₹10 Lakh

Income Level: ₹9 Lakh - ₹10 Lakh

Tax Tip: Use Section 80D and 80G for More Savings

If your income is between ₹9 lakh and ₹10 lakh, you will be taxed at 20% on income above ₹10 lakh. To optimize your tax liability, you should focus on investments that provide both long-term returns and significant tax-saving benefits. The following instruments and deductions can help reduce your taxable income.

Investment Plans

PPF and EPF

Public Provident Fund (PPF) and Employee Provident Fund (EPF) are low-risk, government-backed investment options that offer tax-free returns. Contributions to both of these plans qualify for a tax deduction under Section 80C, thus helping to reduce your taxable income.

NPS Contributions

Contributions to the National Pension System (NPS) are eligible for a tax deduction under Section 80C. Additionally, contributions to NPS under Section 80CCD(1B) allow for an extra tax deduction of up to ₹50,000, over and above the ₹1.5 lakh limit under Section 80C.

Health and Education Savings

Section 80G Donations

Donations made to approved charitable institutions and NGOs are eligible for a tax deduction under Section 80G. The deduction can be either 50% or 100%, depending on the organization. This is a great way to not only reduce taxes but also contribute to a good cause.
