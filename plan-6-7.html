Tax Saving Tips for Income Level ₹6 Lakh - ₹7 Lakh

Income Level: ₹6 Lakh - ₹7 Lakh

Tax Tip: Increase Investments in Tax-Saving Instruments

For individuals earning between ₹6 lakh and ₹7 lakh, you will be taxed at a 5% rate on income above ₹2.5 lakh. To minimize your tax liability, it's essential to increase your investments in various tax-saving instruments available under Sections 80C and 80D. These instruments not only help in reducing taxes but also enable you to build wealth over time.

Tax Saving Options

Tax-Saving Mutual Funds (ELSS)

Equity-linked savings schemes (ELSS) offer tax benefits under Section 80C and come with a 3-year lock-in period. ELSS funds are ideal for those looking for higher returns over the long term. They are suitable for individuals with a moderate-to-high risk appetite.

Home Loan Interest Deduction (Section 24(b))

If you have a home loan, you can claim a deduction on the interest paid under Section 24(b) up to ₹2 lakh, provided the loan is for the purchase or construction of a residential property. This is an excellent option to reduce your taxable income while paying for your home.

Additional Tax Saving Tip

Health Insurance (Section 80D): Ensure you take advantage of Section 80D to deduct premiums paid for health insurance policies. You can claim a deduction for premiums paid for both yourself and your family, up to a limit of ₹25,000 for individuals below 60 years and ₹50,000 for senior citizens.
